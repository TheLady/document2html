◆ m_hasAdditionalSpaceAbove

bool excel::Rowinfo::m_hasAdditionalSpaceAbove = false

This flag is set if the upper border of at least one cell in this row or if the lower border of at least one cell in row above is formatted with a thick line style. Thin and medium line styles are not taken into account

◆ m_hasAdditionalSpaceBelow

bool excel::Rowinfo::m_hasAdditionalSpaceBelow = false

This flag is set if the lower border of at least one cell in this row or if the upper border of at least one cell in row below is formatted with a medium or thick line style. Thin line styles are not taken into account
